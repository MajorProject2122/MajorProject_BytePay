Features

Main Features of BytePay

BytePay is engaged in providing various user-centric & developer friendly features to facilitate convienence, seamlessness and ease of use in various aspects involved in transacting payments.

Device Friendly

BytePay accomodates any browser friendly device and requires no native app installations. It is a responsive platform and adapts to any form factor providing quick access across numerous devices.

Quick Transactions

The principles of simplicity and minimalism is at the core of the platform's functionality providing straighforward and less sophisticated payment methods and procedures resulting in easy and quick transactions.

Blockchain transactions

BytePay makes use of the best of blockchain technology to provide a near fault-free transaction experience. It facilitates cryptocurrency transaction and a blockchain based wallet system for users to be easily able to opt for BytePay as a default payment method.

Safe & Secure

About Us

A blockchain based platform for users to send & receive payments

The main objective behind BytePay is to allow to users to incorporate blockchain based payment systems and currencies for their daily currency exchanges and payments involved in an open market while also providing the benfits of blockchain technology such sophisticated security, easier accounting of transactions with the help of ledger systems and also brief analystics of the payments & the transcations involved.

The platform is designed with the intention to be able to work on any browser friendly device for seamless access and quick payments. BytePay also provides developers with an API service to be able to incorporate BytePay as a payment system and a payment gateway in various thrid party applications to facilitate decentralised transactions.
